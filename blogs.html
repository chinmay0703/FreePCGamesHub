How to Download Free PC Games Safely

Not all "free game downloads" are safe. Follow these **tips to avoid malware and scams**:

  • ✅ Download only from **official game platforms** (Steam, Epic Games, etc.).
  • ✅ Avoid sites offering **"cracked" games** – they often contain malware.
  • ✅ Read **user reviews** before downloading any free game.
  • ✅ Use **antivirus software** and scan downloaded files.
  • ✅ Be careful with **in-game purchases** in "free-to-play" titles.
